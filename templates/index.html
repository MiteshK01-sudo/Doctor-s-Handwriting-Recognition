About

Leveraging AI and ML in healthcare

Leveraging Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Machine Learning (ML) in healthcare is revolutionizing the industry by providing innovative solutions to long-standing problems. One such solution is leveraging AI and ML for doctor's handwritten recognition and E-diagnosis by analyzing symptoms.

Traditionally, doctors use their expertise and experience to diagnose patients based on their symptoms, medical history, and physical examination. However, with the advent of AI and ML, it is now possible to analyze large amounts of data to recognize doctors' handwritten notes and diagnose patients accurately based on their symptoms.
